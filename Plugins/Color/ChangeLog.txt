-------------------------------
  ij-plugins Color Change Log
-------------------------------


v.1.0
-----

* Plugin "Color Histogram" moved to module ij-VTK to remove dependency on VTK.

* New plugins:
  - RGB to CIE L*a*b* stack
  - CIE L*a*b* stack to RGB
  - Multiband Sobel edges
  - Intensity shift

* plugins JAR name changed from ij_color.jar to ij-plugins_color.jar
